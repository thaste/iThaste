Strategy is a Course of action that is an element of a plan devised through the science and art of business leadership exercised to ensure the most advantageous conditions. Compared to a Tactic, a Strategy tend(s) to be longer term and is broader in scope. Strategy is the science and art of military command exercised to meet the enemy in combat under advantageous conditions. Strategy is a careful plan or method or a clever stratagem.

 

Strategy is a component of the plan for a Mission.

Strategy may determine Organization unit(s).

Tactic(s) implements Strategy.
