Problem Statement

iThaste is an initiative of the company, Thaste IT, to deliver added value to their customer by giving them the opportunity to govern all Quality related matters with an Opensource tool.

iThaste is build in Java, using the Play Framework version 2.0, combining Scala and Java to obtain the best result.

Thaste It and its customers are Quality minded companies, which means that they aim for quality in what they do, how they do it, why they do it, etc.

Thaste IT gives support to their customers and delivers services and products to them, with the aim to continuously increase the maturity and the capability to deliver software products of sufficient Quality.

Although Quality on itself is not a business reason, it surely is a mean
